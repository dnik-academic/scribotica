Our Mission

We founded Writers Analytica with the mission to improve your business through modern analytics. We aim to provide all customers with research-based information on linguistic practices of most successful companies in their field and provide solutions. Generative AI is not magical. It needs to be directed and controlled. We use it merely as a great tool, but WE are the ones making decisions and offering solutions.

Core Values

We achieve integrity by delivering reliable and research-based information to our clients. We focus on innovation, teamwork, social responsibility, and professionalism in our approaches.

Our CEO

Dr Dušan Nikolić has a Ph.D. in Linguistics received at the University of Calgary, in Canada. He is an expert in linguistic analysis and has many years of experience working in industry as a data analyst.
